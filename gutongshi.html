Gu Tong Shi

古铜石

This is a special kind of stone. It comes from Guizhou Province, China. The stone grows symbiotically with the coal mine in a coal mine. Because its stone quality and color are similar to ancient Chinese bronzes, it is called Gu Tong stone (bronze stone). This is a relatively rare stone.
